Sunnah Practices

Complete guide to following the prophetic traditions and daily practices of Prophet Muhammad sallallahu alayhi wa sallam

Understanding Sunnah

The Sunnah refers to the way of life, practices, and traditions of Prophet Muhammad sallallahu alayhi wa sallam. It includes his sayings, actions, approvals, and characteristics. Following the Sunnah is a way to draw closer to Allah and earn His pleasure.

Quranic Foundation
لَقَدْ كَانَ لَكُمْ فِي رَسُولِ اللَّهِ أُسْوَةٌ حَسَنَةٌ لِمَنْ كَانَ يَرْجُو اللَّهَ وَالْيَوْمَ الْآخِرَ
"There has certainly been for you in the Messenger of Allah an excellent pattern for anyone whose hope is in Allah and the Last Day." (Quran 33:21)

Daily Sunnah Practices

1. Morning and Evening Remembrance

Start and end your day with Islamic remembrance:

أَصْبَحْنَا وَأَصْبَحَ الْمُلْكُ لِلَّهِ وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ
"We have reached the morning and at this very time all sovereignty belongs to Allah. All praise is for Allah."

Evening version: Replace "asbahna" with "amsayna" (we have reached the evening).

Benefits
  • Protection throughout the day
  • Remembrance of Allah's sovereignty
  • Expression of gratitude
  • Spiritual protection and blessings

2. Before Eating and Drinking

Always begin meals with the proper remembrance:

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ
"In the name of Allah"

After eating: Say "Alhamdulillah" (Praise be to Allah)

Prophetic Teaching

The Prophet sallallahu alayhi wa sallam said: "When one of you eats, let him mention the name of Allah. If he forgets to mention the name of Allah at the beginning, let him say: 'In the name of Allah at the beginning and at the end.'"

3. Entering and Leaving Home

Proper etiquette when entering and leaving:

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ عَلَى اللَّهِ وَلَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللَّهِ
"In the name of Allah, I place my trust in Allah, and there is no power and no strength except with Allah."

When leaving: Add "Allahumma inni a'udhu bika an adilla aw udalla, aw azilla aw uzalla, aw azlima aw uzlima, aw ajhala aw yujhala 'alayya"

4. Entering and Leaving the Mosque

Proper etiquette for places of worship:

اللَّهُمَّ افْتَحْ لِي أَبْوَابَ رَحْمَتِكَ
"O Allah, open for me the gates of Your mercy."

When leaving: Say "Allahumma inni as'aluka min fadlika" (O Allah, I ask You of Your bounty)

5. Sneezing and Yawning

Proper responses to natural actions:

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ
"Praise be to Allah" (when sneezing)

Response: "Yarhamuk Allah" (May Allah have mercy on you)

For yawning: Try to suppress it, and if you must yawn, cover your mouth

Personal Hygiene Sunnah

1. Using the Miswak (Tooth Stick)

Regular oral hygiene practice:

  • Frequency: Before prayers and when needed
  • Benefits: Clean mouth, fresh breath, spiritual reward
  • Method: Clean teeth and gums gently
  • Alternative: Toothbrush if miswak unavailable
Prophetic Teaching

The Prophet sallallahu alayhi wa sallam said: "The miswak is a means of purifying the mouth and pleasing the Lord."

2. Trimming Nails and Mustache

Regular grooming practices:

  • Nails: Trim regularly, preferably on Fridays
  • Mustache: Keep trimmed and neat
  • Beard: Let grow naturally (for men)
  • Hair: Keep clean and well-groomed

3. Wearing Perfume

Maintaining pleasant fragrance:

  • For men: Use perfume when going out
  • For women: Use perfume at home, not in public
  • Types: Natural fragrances preferred
  • Moderation: Don't overuse

Social Sunnah Practices

1. Greeting with Salam

Proper Islamic greetings:

السَّلَامُ عَلَيْكُمْ
"Peace be upon you"

Response: "Wa alaykum as-salam" (And upon you be peace)

Benefits
  • Spreads peace and love
  • Earns reward from Allah
  • Strengthens community bonds
  • Follows prophetic example

2. Visiting the Sick

Showing care and compassion:

  • Frequency: Visit when possible
  • Duration: Keep visits short and beneficial
  • Actions: Make dua, offer help, show concern
  • Benefits: Earns reward and strengthens bonds
Prophetic Teaching

The Prophet sallallahu alayhi wa sallam said: "When you visit a sick person, ask him to make dua for you, for his dua is like the dua of angels."

3. Attending Funerals

Showing respect for the deceased:

  • Participation: Attend when possible
  • Behavior: Show respect and solemnity
  • Dua: Make sincere dua for the deceased
  • Reflection: Remember your own mortality

Business and Financial Sunnah

1. Honest Business Practices

Maintaining integrity in business:

  • Truthfulness: Always be honest about products and services
  • Fair Pricing: Don't overcharge or undercut unfairly
  • Quality: Provide the best possible service
  • Transparency: Be clear about terms and conditions
  • Charity: Give from your business earnings

2. Avoiding Interest (Riba)

Islamic financial principles:

  • Definition: Avoid all forms of interest
  • Alternatives: Use interest-free financial products
  • Investments: Choose halal investment options
  • Banking: Use Islamic banking services when available

3. Paying Workers Promptly

Fair treatment of employees:

  • Timely Payment: Pay wages when due
  • Fair Wages: Pay what is deserved
  • Respect: Treat workers with dignity
  • Benefits: Provide fair benefits when possible

Family and Home Sunnah

1. Family Meals

Building family bonds through food:

  • Togetherness: Eat together when possible
  • Sharing: Share food with family members
  • Gratitude: Express thanks for the meal
  • Moderation: Don't overeat

2. Visiting Family

Maintaining family relationships:

  • Regular Visits: Visit family members regularly
  • Gifts: Bring small gifts when visiting
  • Time: Spend quality time together
  • Support: Offer help when needed

3. Home Environment

Creating a positive home atmosphere:

  • Cleanliness: Keep home clean and organized
  • Islamic Reminders: Display Islamic calligraphy or verses
  • Prayer Area: Designate a clean prayer space
  • Positive Atmosphere: Maintain peace and harmony

Benefits of Following Sunnah

Spiritual Benefits

  • Closer to Allah: Following the Prophet's example draws us closer to Allah
  • Increased Reward: Sunnah practices earn additional rewards
  • Protection: Many sunnah practices provide spiritual protection
  • Blessings: Following sunnah brings barakah (blessings)

Practical Benefits

  • Better Health: Many sunnah practices promote good health
  • Stronger Relationships: Social sunnah practices build bonds
  • Improved Character: Following sunnah develops good character
  • Community Harmony: Sunnah practices create social harmony
