Daily Life Sunnah
Morning to Evening Routines

Morning Sunnah

Wake up early, say morning dhikr, pray Fajr, make dua for the day

Eating Sunnah

Start with Bismillah, eat with right hand, eat from your side of the plate

Sleeping Sunnah

Sleep on right side, recite Ayat al-Kursi, make dua before sleeping

Bathroom Etiquette

Enter with left foot, exit with right foot, avoid speaking, make dua

Prophet's Teaching

"Whoever says 'Subhan Allah' thirty-three times, 'Alhamdulillah' thirty-three times, and 'Allahu Akbar' thirty-four times in the morning, and does the same in the evening, will have a complete reward."
Sahih Bukhari
