Sunan Ibn Majah

Collection of authentic Hadiths compiled by Muhammad ibn Yazid (824-887 CE)

About Sunan Ibn Majah

Sunan Ibn Majah is one of the six major Hadith collections (Kutub al-Sittah) in Sunni Islam. Compiled by Muhammad ibn Yazid ibn Majah al-Qazwini, this collection contains approximately 4,341 Hadiths and completes the six major Hadith books.

The collection covers a wide range of topics including faith, worship, social relations, and legal rulings. While some Hadiths in this collection have been criticized for their authenticity, it remains an important reference for scholars and students of Islamic studies.
