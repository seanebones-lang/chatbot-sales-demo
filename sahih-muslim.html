About Sahih Muslim

Sahih Muslim is the second most authentic collection of Hadiths in Sunni Islam, compiled by Imam Muslim ibn al-Hajjaj. He was a student of Imam al-Bukhari and applied similarly rigorous standards for authenticity.

The collection contains over 7,500 Hadiths and is particularly known for its excellent organization and the quality of its chains of narration. Imam Muslim focused on presenting Hadiths with complete chains and avoided repetition, making it a valuable complement to Sahih al-Bukhari.

May Allah guide us to understand and implement these authentic teachings
