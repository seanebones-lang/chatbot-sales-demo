Authentic Duas

Supplications from Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) - Daily Prayers and Remembrances

Introduction to Duas

What are Duas?

Duas (supplications) are personal prayers and invocations to Allah. They are a direct means of communication between a believer and their Creator. Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) taught us numerous duas for various occasions and situations in life. These authentic supplications help us remember Allah throughout the day and seek His guidance, protection, and blessings.

How to Use These Duas

Learn these duas gradually, starting with the most commonly used ones. Recite them with understanding and sincerity. It's better to learn a few duas well and use them regularly than to memorize many without understanding their meaning. Remember that the most important aspect is the sincerity of your heart when making these supplications.

Daily Morning and Evening Duas

Morning Supplication (Dua for Starting the Day)

أَصْبَحْنَا وَأَصْبَحَ الْمُلْكُ لِلَّهِ، وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ، لاَ إِلَهَ إِلاَّ اللَّهُ وَحْدَهُ لاَ شَرِيكَ لَهُ، لَهُ الْمُلْكُ وَلَهُ الْحَمْدُ وَهُوَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ
Asbahna wa asbahal mulku lillah, walhamdu lillah, la ilaha illallahu wahdahu la shareeka lah, lahul mulku wa lahul hamdu wa huwa 'ala kulli shay'in qadeer
We have reached the morning and at this very time all sovereignty belongs to Allah. All praise is for Allah. None has the right to be worshipped except Allah, alone, without any partner, to Him belongs all sovereignty and praise and He is over all things omnipotent.
Reference: Muslim 4/2088
Benefits:

This dua helps start the day with remembrance of Allah and acknowledgment of His sovereignty. It's a powerful way to begin each morning with the right mindset.

Evening Supplication (Dua for Ending the Day)

أَمْسَيْنَا وَأَمْسَى الْمُلْكُ لِلَّهِ، وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ، لاَ إِلَهَ إِلاَّ اللَّهُ وَحْدَهُ لاَ شَرِيكَ لَهُ، لَهُ الْمُلْكُ وَلَهُ الْحَمْدُ وَهُوَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ
Amsayna wa amsal mulku lillah, walhamdu lillah, la ilaha illallahu wahdahu la shareeka lah, lahul mulku wa lahul hamdu wa huwa 'ala kulli shay'in qadeer
We have reached the evening and at this very time all sovereignty belongs to Allah. All praise is for Allah. None has the right to be worshipped except Allah, alone, without any partner, to Him belongs all sovereignty and praise and He is over all things omnipotent.
Reference: Muslim 4/2088
Benefits:

Reciting this dua in the evening helps reflect on the day and end it with gratitude and remembrance of Allah.

Morning Remembrance (Subhan Allah)

سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ وَبِحَمْدِهِ
Subhanallahi wa bihamdihi
Glory be to Allah and His is the praise
Reference: Muslim 4/2073
Benefits:

Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) said that saying this 100 times in the morning and evening will erase all sins, even if they are as much as the foam of the sea.

Evening Remembrance (La ilaha illallah)

لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ، لَهُ الْمُلْكُ وَلَهُ الْحَمْدُ وَهُوَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ
La ilaha illallahu wahdahu la shareeka lah, lahul mulku wa lahul hamdu wa huwa 'ala kulli shay'in qadeer
None has the right to be worshipped except Allah, alone, without any partner, to Him belongs all sovereignty and praise and He is over all things omnipotent.
Reference: Bukhari 3293
Benefits:

Reciting this 100 times in the morning and evening provides protection and brings great reward.

Duas for Prayer and Worship

Before Starting Prayer

اللَّهُمَّ بَاعِدْ بَيْنِي وَبَيْنَ خَطَايَايَ كَمَا بَاعَدْتَ بَيْنَ الْمَشْرِقِ وَالْمَغْرِبِ، اللَّهُمَّ نَقِّنِي مِنْ خَطَايَايَ كَمَا يُنَقَّى الثَّوْبُ الأَبْيَضُ مِنَ الدَّنَسِ
Allahumma ba'id bayni wa bayna khatayaya kama ba'adta baynal-mashriqi wal-maghrib. Allahumma naqqini min khatayaya kama yunaqqath-thawbul-abyadu minad-danas
O Allah, distance me from my sins as You have distanced the east from the west. O Allah, purify me of my sins as a white garment is purified of filth.
Reference: Bukhari 744
Benefits:

This dua helps purify the heart before prayer and seeks Allah's forgiveness for sins.

After Prayer (Tasbih)

سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ (33 times) - الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ (33 times) - اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ (34 times)
Subhanallah (33 times) - Alhamdulillah (33 times) - Allahu Akbar (34 times)
Glory be to Allah (33 times) - All praise is for Allah (33 times) - Allah is the Greatest (34 times)
Reference: Muslim 4/2073
Benefits:

Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) said that whoever recites these after each prayer will have all their sins forgiven.

Dua for Qunut (Witr Prayer)

اللَّهُمَّ اهْدِنِي فِيمَنْ هَدَيْتَ، وَعَافِنِي فِيمَنْ عَافَيْتَ، وَتَوَلَّنِي فِيمَنْ تَوَلَّيْتَ، وَبَارِكْ لِي فِيمَا أَعْطَيْتَ، وَقِنِي شَرَّ مَا قَضَيْتَ
Allahumma-hdini fiman hadayta, wa 'afini fiman 'afayta, wa tawallani fiman tawallayta, wa barik li fima a'tayta, wa qini sharra ma qadayta
O Allah, guide me among those whom You have guided, pardon me among those whom You have pardoned, befriend me among those whom You have befriended, bless me in what You have granted, and protect me from the evil that You decreed.
Reference: Abu Dawud 1425
Benefits:

This comprehensive dua seeks Allah's guidance, pardon, friendship, blessings, and protection.

Duas for Daily Activities

Before Eating

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ
Bismillah
In the name of Allah
Reference: Bukhari 5376
Benefits:

This simple dua reminds us that all sustenance comes from Allah and helps us eat with mindfulness and gratitude.

After Eating

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَطْعَمَنِي هَذَا وَرَزَقَنِيهِ مِنْ غَيْرِ حَوْلٍ مِنِّي وَلاَ قُوَّةٍ
Alhamdu lillahil-ladhi at'amani hadha wa razaqanihi min ghayri hawlin minni wa la quwwah
All praise is for Allah who gave me this food and provided it for me, without any strength or power on my part.
Reference: Abu Dawud 4023
Benefits:

This dua teaches gratitude and acknowledges that all provisions come from Allah's mercy.

Before Entering Home

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ وَلَجْنَا، وَبِسْمِ اللَّهِ خَرَجْنَا، وَعَلَى اللَّهِ رَبِّنَا تَوَكَّلْنَا
Bismillahi walajna, wa bismillahi kharajna, wa 'alallahi rabbina tawakkalna
In the name of Allah we enter, in the name of Allah we leave, and upon our Lord we place our trust.
Reference: Abu Dawud 5096
Benefits:

This dua seeks Allah's protection when entering and leaving the home, placing trust in Him for safety.

Before Sleeping

بِاسْمِكَ رَبِّي وَضَعْتُ جَنْبِي، وَبِكَ أَرْفَعُهُ، فَإِنْ أَمْسَكْتَ نَفْسِي فَارْحَمْهَا، وَإِنْ أَرْسَلْتَهَا فَاحْفَظْهَا بِمَا تَحْفَظُ بِهِ عِبَادَكَ الصَّالِحِينَ
Bismika rabbi wada'tu janbi, wa bika arfa'uhu, fa in amsakta nafsi farhamha, wa in arsaltaha fahfazha bima tahfazu bihi 'ibadakas-saliheen
In Your name, my Lord, I lay down my side, and by You I raise it. If You should take my soul then have mercy upon it, and if You should return my soul then protect it in the manner You do so for Your righteous servants.
Reference: Bukhari 6320
Benefits:

This dua seeks Allah's protection during sleep and places our trust in Him for our safety and well-being.

Before Entering the Bathroom

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْخُبُثِ وَالْخَبَائِثِ
Allahumma inni a'udhu bika minal-khubuthi wal-khaba'ith
O Allah, I seek refuge in You from evil and from evil doers (male and female devils).
Reference: Bukhari 142
Benefits:

This dua seeks protection from evil spirits and maintains spiritual cleanliness.

After Leaving the Bathroom

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَذْهَبَ عَنِّي الأَذَى وَعَافَانِي
Alhamdulillahil-ladhi adh-haba 'annil-adha wa 'afani
Praise be to Allah who has removed from me discomfort and granted me relief.
Reference: Abu Dawud 30
Benefits:

This dua expresses gratitude for the relief and comfort Allah has provided.

Before Looking in the Mirror

اللَّهُمَّ كَمَا حَسَّنْتَ خَلْقِي فَحَسِّنْ خُلُقِي
Allahumma kama hassanta khalqi fa hassin khuluqi
O Allah, as You have made my appearance beautiful, make my character beautiful as well.
Reference: Common Islamic practice
Benefits:

This dua reminds us to focus on inner beauty and good character, not just outward appearance.

Before Dressing

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي كَسَانِي هَذَا وَرَزَقَنِيهِ مِنْ غَيْرِ حَوْلٍ مِنِّي وَلاَ قُوَّةٍ
Alhamdulillahil-ladhi kasani hadha wa razaqanihi min ghayri hawlin minni wa la quwwah
All praise is for Allah who has clothed me with this and provided it for me, with no power or might from myself.
Reference: Abu Dawud 4023
Benefits:

This dua teaches gratitude for clothing and acknowledges Allah as the provider of all blessings.

Duas for Health and Healing

Dua for Seeking Treatment

اللَّهُمَّ اشْفِ مَرِيضِي، اللَّهُمَّ اشْفِ مَرِيضِي، اللَّهُمَّ اشْفِ مَرِيضِي
Allahumma-shfi mareedi, Allahumma-shfi mareedi, Allahumma-shfi mareedi
O Allah, heal my patient, O Allah, heal my patient, O Allah, heal my patient.
Reference: Abu Dawud 3106
Benefits:

Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) used this dua when visiting the sick, seeking Allah's healing for them.

Dua for Pain Relief

أَذْهِبِ الْبَأْسَ رَبَّ النَّاسِ، اشْفِ أَنْتَ الشَّافِي، لَا شِفَاءَ إِلَّا شِفَاؤُكَ، شِفَاءً لَا يُغَادِرُ سَقَمًا
Adh-hibil-ba'sa rabban-nas, ishfi antash-shafi, la shifa'a illa shifa'uka, shifa'an la yughadiru saqaman
Remove the harm, O Lord of mankind, and heal, for You are the Healer. There is no healing except Your healing, a healing that leaves no illness.
Reference: Bukhari 5742
Benefits:

This powerful dua seeks Allah's healing and acknowledges that He is the ultimate Healer.

Dua for Visiting the Sick

لَا بَأْسَ طَهُورٌ إِنْ شَاءَ اللَّهُ
La ba'sa tahurun in sha'allah
No harm, (it will be) purification, if Allah wills.
Reference: Bukhari 3616
Benefits:

This dua comforts the sick person and reminds them that illness can be a means of purification.

Dua for Mental Peace

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْهَمِّ وَالْحَزَنِ، وَأَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْعَجْزِ وَالْكَسَلِ، وَأَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْجُبْنِ وَالْبُخْلِ، وَأَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ غَلَبَةِ الدَّيْنِ وَقَهْرِ الرِّجَالِ
Allahumma inni a'udhu bika minal-hammi wal-hazan, wa a'udhu bika minal-'ajzi wal-kasal, wa a'udhu bika minal-jubni wal-bukhl, wa a'udhu bika min ghalabatid-dayni wa qahrir-rijal
O Allah, I seek refuge in You from anxiety and sorrow, weakness and laziness, miserliness and cowardice, the burden of debts and from being over powered by men.
Reference: Bukhari 6369
Benefits:

This comprehensive dua seeks protection from various mental and emotional difficulties.

Duas for Protection and Safety

Ayat al-Kursi (The Throne Verse)

اللَّهُ لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ الْحَيُّ الْقَيُّومُ ۚ لَا تَأْخُذُهُ سِنَةٌ وَلَا نَوْمٌ ۚ لَّهُ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَمَا فِي الْأَرْضِ ۗ مَن ذَا الَّذِي يَشْفَعُ عِندَهُ إِلَّا بِإِذْنِهِ ۚ يَعْلَمُ مَا بَيْنَ أَيْدِيهِمْ وَمَا خَلْفَهُمْ ۖ وَلَا يُحِيطُونَ بِشَيْءٍ مِّنْ عِلْمِهِ إِلَّا بِمَا شَاءَ ۚ وَسِعَ كُرْسِيُّهُ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضَ ۖ وَلَا يَئُودُهُ حِفْظُهُمَا ۚ وَهُوَ الْعَلِيُّ الْعَظِيمُ
Allahu la ilaha illa huwal-hayyul-qayyum. La ta'khudhuhu sinatuw-wa la nawm. Lahu ma fis-samawati wa ma fil-ard. Man dhal-ladhi yashfa'u 'indahu illa bi-idhnih. Ya'lamu ma bayna aydeehim wa ma khalfahum. Wa la yuhituna bi-shay'in min 'ilmihi illa bima sha'. Wasi'a kursiyyuhus-samawati wal-ard, wa la ya'uduhu hifdhuhuma. Wa huwal-'aliyyul-'adheem
Allah - there is no deity except Him, the Ever-Living, the Self-Sustaining. Neither drowsiness overtakes Him nor sleep. To Him belongs whatever is in the heavens and whatever is on the earth. Who is it that can intercede with Him except by His permission? He knows what is [presently] before them and what will be after them, and they encompass not a thing of His knowledge except for what He wills. His Kursi extends over the heavens and the earth, and their preservation tires Him not. And He is the Most High, the Most Great.
Reference: Quran 2:255
Benefits:

Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) said that whoever recites this verse in the morning will be protected until evening, and whoever recites it in the evening will be protected until morning.

Dua for Protection from Evil

أَعُوذُ بِكَلِمَاتِ اللَّهِ التَّامَّاتِ الَّتِي لَا يُجَاوِزُهُنَّ بَرٌّ وَلَا فَاجِرٌ
A'udhu bi kalimatillahit-tammatil-lati la yujawizuhunna barrun wa la fajir
I seek refuge in the perfect words of Allah which neither the upright nor the corrupt can overcome.
Reference: Muslim 2708
Benefits:

This dua provides protection from various forms of harm and evil, seeking refuge in Allah's perfect words.

Dua for Protection from Shaytan

أَعُوذُ بِاللَّهِ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيمِ
A'udhu billahi minash-shaytanir-rajeem
I seek refuge in Allah from the accursed Satan.
Reference: Quran 16:98
Benefits:

This dua is recited before reading Quran and seeking protection from Satan's influence.

Dua for Protection from Magic

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ أَرْقِيكَ، مِنْ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ يُؤْذِيكَ، مِنْ شَرِّ كُلِّ نَفْسٍ أَوْ عَيْنِ حَاسِدٍ، اللَّهُ يَشْفِيكَ، بِسْمِ اللَّهِ أَرْقِيكَ
Bismillahi arqik, min kulli shay'in yu'dhik, min sharri kulli nafsin aw 'aynin hasid, Allahu yashfik, bismillahi arqik
In the name of Allah I perform ruqyah for you, from everything that harms you, from the evil of every soul or envious eye, may Allah heal you, in the name of Allah I perform ruqyah for you.
Reference: Muslim 2186
Benefits:

This dua is used for spiritual healing and protection from evil eye and magic.

Duas for Seeking Forgiveness

Dua for Seeking Forgiveness (Sayyid al-Istighfar)

اللَّهُمَّ أَنْتَ رَبِّي لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ خَلَقْتَنِي وَأَنَا عَبْدُكَ وَأَنَا عَلَى عَهْدِكَ وَوَعْدِكَ مَا اسْتَطَعْتُ أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا صَنَعْتُ أَبُوءُ لَكَ بِنِعْمَتِكَ عَلَيَّ وَأَبُوءُ لَكَ بِذَنْبِي فَاغْفِرْ لِي فَإِنَّهُ لَا يَغْفِرُ الذُّنُوبَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ
Allahumma anta rabbi la ilaha illa anta, khalaqtani wa ana 'abduka, wa ana 'ala 'ahdika wa wa'dika mastata'tu. A'udhu bika min sharri ma sana'tu, abu'u laka bi ni'matika 'alayya, wa abu'u laka bi dhanbi, faghfir li fa innahu la yaghfirudh-dhunuba illa anta
O Allah, You are my Lord, none has the right to be worshipped except You, You created me and I am Your servant, and I abide to Your covenant and promise as best I can, I take refuge in You from the evil of which I have committed. I acknowledge Your favor upon me and I acknowledge my sin, so forgive me, for verily none can forgive sin except You.
Reference: Bukhari 6306
Benefits:

Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) said that whoever recites this dua with conviction in the morning and dies before evening, or recites it in the evening and dies before morning, will enter Paradise.

Simple Istighfar

أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللَّهَ
Astaghfirullah
I seek forgiveness from Allah
Reference: Common Islamic practice
Benefits:

This simple dua can be recited frequently throughout the day to seek Allah's forgiveness and maintain a connection with Him.

Duas for Business and Work

Dua Before Starting Work

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ خَيْرَهَا وَخَيْرَ مَا فِيهَا، وَأَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ شَرِّهَا وَشَرِّ مَا فِيهَا
Allahumma inni as'aluka khayraha wa khayra ma fiha, wa a'udhu bika min sharriha wa sharri ma fiha
O Allah, I ask You for the best of it and the best of what is in it, and I seek refuge in You from the evil of it and the evil of what is in it.
Reference: Abu Dawud 2606
Benefits:

This dua seeks Allah's blessings for the day's work and protection from any harm.

Dua for Business Success

اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لِي فِي مَكْسَبِي، وَاجْعَلْهُ حَلَالًا طَيِّبًا، وَلَا تَجْعَلْ حِرْمَانَهُ حِرْمَانَ رَحْمَةٍ مِنْكَ
Allahumma barik li fi maksabi, waj'alhu halalan tayyiban, wa la taj'al hirmanahu hirmana rahmatin minka
O Allah, bless me in my earnings, make them lawful and pure, and do not let the deprivation of them be a deprivation of Your mercy.
Reference: Common Islamic practice
Benefits:

This dua asks for Allah's blessings in business and ensures earnings remain halal and blessed.

Dua for Entering the Market

لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ، لَهُ الْمُلْكُ وَلَهُ الْحَمْدُ يُحْيِي وَيُمِيتُ وَهُوَ حَيٌّ لَا يَمُوتُ، بِيَدِهِ الْخَيْرُ وَهُوَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ
La ilaha illallahu wahdahu la shareeka lah, lahul mulku wa lahul hamdu yuhyi wa yumeet wa huwa hayyun la yamoot, bi yadihil khayru wa huwa 'ala kulli shay'in qadeer
None has the right to be worshipped except Allah, alone, without any partner, to Him belongs all sovereignty and praise, He gives life and causes death, and He is Ever Living and does not die, in His Hand is all good, and He is Able to do all things.
Reference: Tirmidhi 3427
Benefits:

This dua is recited when entering the market to seek Allah's blessings and protection.

Duas for Knowledge and Wisdom

Dua for Seeking Knowledge

رَبِّ زِدْنِي عِلْمًا
Rabbi zidni 'ilma
My Lord, increase me in knowledge
Reference: Quran 20:114
Benefits:

This dua from the Quran teaches us to always seek more knowledge and understanding, recognizing that learning is a continuous process.

Dua for Beneficial Knowledge

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ عِلْمًا نَافِعًا، وَرِزْقًا طَيِّبًا، وَعَمَلًا مُتَقَبَّلًا
Allahumma inni as'aluka 'ilman nafi'an, wa rizqan tayyiban, wa 'amalan mutaqabbalan
O Allah, I ask You for beneficial knowledge, good provision, and acceptable deeds.
Reference: Ibn Majah 925
Benefits:

This comprehensive dua asks for knowledge that benefits us, sustenance that is pure, and actions that are accepted by Allah.

Dua Before Studying

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ فَهْمَ النَّبِيِّينَ، وَحِفْظَ الْمُرْسَلِينَ، وَإِلْهَامَ الْمَلَائِكَةِ الْمُقَرَّبِينَ
Allahumma inni as'aluka fahman-nabiyyeen, wa hifzal-mursaleen, wa ilhamal-mala'ikatil-muqarrabeen
O Allah, I ask You for the understanding of the prophets, the memory of the messengers, and the inspiration of the angels who are brought near.
Reference: Common Islamic practice
Benefits:

This dua seeks Allah's help in understanding, memorizing, and being inspired in learning.

Duas for Travel and Journey

Dua for Traveling

اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّا نَسْأَلُكَ فِي سَفَرِنَا هَذَا الْبِرَّ وَالتَّقْوَى، وَمِنَ الْعَمَلِ مَا تَرْضَى، اللَّهُمَّ هَوِّنْ عَلَيْنَا سَفَرَنَا هَذَا وَاطْوِ عَنَّا بُعْدَهُ، اللَّهُمَّ أَنْتَ الصَّاحِبُ فِي السَّفَرِ، وَالْخَلِيفَةُ فِي الْأَهْلِ
Allahumma inna nas'aluka fi safarina hadha al-birra wat-taqwa, wa minal-'amali ma tardha. Allahumma hawwin 'alayna safarana hadha watwi 'anna bu'dahu. Allahumma antas-sahibu fis-safari, wal-khalifatu fil-ahli
O Allah, we ask You on this journey of ours for righteousness, piety, and such deeds as are pleasing to You. O Allah, make this journey easy for us and fold up for us its distance. O Allah, You are the Companion on the journey and the One Who looks after the family.
Reference: Muslim 1342
Benefits:

This dua seeks Allah's protection during travel and asks for His help in making the journey easy and safe.

Dua for Boarding a Vehicle

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ
Bismillah
In the name of Allah
Reference: Abu Dawud 3832
Benefits:

This simple dua seeks Allah's protection when traveling by any means of transportation.

Dua for Returning from Travel

آيِبُونَ، تَائِبُونَ، عَابِدُونَ، لِرَبِّنَا حَامِدُونَ
Ayibuna, ta'ibuna, 'abiduna, li rabbina hamidun
We return, we repent, we worship, and we praise our Lord.
Reference: Muslim 1342
Benefits:

This dua expresses gratitude for safe return and reaffirms commitment to worship and repentance.

Duas for Special Occasions

Dua for New Moon

اللَّهُمَّ أَهِلَّهُ عَلَيْنَا بِالْأَمْنِ وَالْإِيمَانِ، وَالسَّلَامَةِ وَالْإِسْلَامِ، رَبِّي وَرَبُّكَ اللَّهُ
Allahumma ahillahu 'alayna bil-amni wal-iman, was-salamati wal-Islam, rabbi wa rabbukal-lah
O Allah, make it rise over us with security and faith, safety and Islam. My Lord and your Lord is Allah.
Reference: Tirmidhi 3451
Benefits:

This dua is recited when sighting the new moon, seeking Allah's blessings for the new month.

Dua for Entering a New Home

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ خَيْرَ الْمَوْلَجِ وَخَيْرَ الْمَخْرَجِ، بِسْمِ اللَّهِ وَلَجْنَا، وَبِسْمِ اللَّهِ خَرَجْنَا، وَعَلَى اللَّهِ رَبِّنَا تَوَكَّلْنَا
Allahumma inni as'aluka khayral-mawlaji wa khayral-makhraji, bismillahi walajna, wa bismillahi kharajna, wa 'alallahi rabbina tawakkalna
O Allah, I ask You for the best of entry and the best of exit. In the name of Allah we enter, in the name of Allah we leave, and upon our Lord we place our trust.
Reference: Abu Dawud 5096
Benefits:

This dua seeks Allah's blessings for the new home and protection for all who enter and leave.

Dua for Wedding

بَارَكَ اللَّهُ لَكَ وَبَارَكَ عَلَيْكَ وَجَمَعَ بَيْنَكُمَا فِي خَيْرٍ
Barakallahu laka wa baraka 'alayka wa jama'a baynakuma fi khayr
May Allah bless you and shower His blessings upon you, and may He join you together in goodness.
Reference: Abu Dawud 2130
Benefits:

This dua is recited to congratulate newlyweds and seek Allah's blessings for their marriage.

Duas for Family and Relationships

Dua for Children

اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لِي فِي أَوْلَادِي وَلاَ تَضُرَّهُمْ، وَوَفِّقْهُمْ لِطَاعَتِكَ وَارْزُقْنِي بِرَّهُمْ
Allahumma barik li fi awladi wa la tadurrahum, wa waffiqhum li ta'atika warzuqni birrahum
O Allah, bless me in my children and do not cause them harm, guide them to obey You and grant me their righteousness.
Reference: Common Islamic practice
Benefits:

This dua asks for Allah's blessings on children and seeks their guidance and righteousness, which brings joy to parents.

Dua for Parents

رَبِّ ارْحَمْهُمَا كَمَا رَبَّيَانِي صَغِيرًا
Rabbi irhamhuma kama rabbayani saghira
My Lord, have mercy upon them as they brought me up when I was small.
Reference: Quran 17:24
Benefits:

This dua from the Quran teaches us to pray for our parents' mercy, recognizing their care and sacrifice in raising us.

Dua for Spouse

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ خَيْرَهَا وَخَيْرَ مَا جَبَلْتَهَا عَلَيْهِ، وَأَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ شَرِّهَا وَشَرِّ مَا جَبَلْتَهَا عَلَيْهِ
Allahumma inni as'aluka khayraha wa khayra ma jabaltaha 'alayhi, wa a'udhu bika min sharriha wa sharri ma jabaltaha 'alayhi
O Allah, I ask You for the best of her and the best of that which You have made her inclined towards, and I seek refuge in You from the evil of her and the evil of that which You have made her inclined towards.
Reference: Abu Dawud 2160
Benefits:

This dua seeks Allah's blessings for the spouse and protection from any harm in the relationship.

Duas for Emotional and Spiritual Well-being

Dua for Patience

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ الصَّبْرَ عَلَى مَا أَحْبَبْتُ، وَالصَّبْرَ عَلَى مَا كَرِهْتُ، وَالصَّبْرَ عَلَى مَا أَبْغَضْتُ
Allahumma inni as'alukas-sabra 'ala ma ahababtu, was-sabra 'ala ma karihtu, was-sabra 'ala ma abghadtu
O Allah, I ask You for patience over what I love, patience over what I dislike, and patience over what I detest.
Reference: Common Islamic practice
Benefits:

This dua seeks Allah's help in developing patience in all situations, whether pleasant or difficult.

Dua for Gratitude

اللَّهُمَّ لَكَ الْحَمْدُ حَتَّى تَرْضَى، وَلَكَ الْحَمْدُ إِذَا رَضِيتَ، وَلَكَ الْحَمْدُ بَعْدَ الرِّضَا
Allahumma lakal-hamdu hatta tardha, wa lakal-hamdu idha radita, wa lakal-hamdu ba'dar-rida
O Allah, to You is all praise until You are pleased, and to You is all praise when You are pleased, and to You is all praise after You are pleased.
Reference: Ibn Majah 3800
Benefits:

This dua expresses continuous gratitude to Allah in all circumstances and states.

Dua for Contentment

اللَّهُمَّ قَنِّعْنِي بِمَا رَزَقْتَنِي، وَبَارِكْ لِي فِيهِ، وَاخْلُفْ عَلَيَّ كُلَّ غَائِبَةٍ لِي بِخَيْرٍ
Allahumma qanni'ni bima razaqtani, wa barik li fihi, wakhluf 'alayya kulla gha'ibatin li bi khayr
O Allah, make me content with what You have provided me, bless me in it, and replace for me every loss with something better.
Reference: Common Islamic practice
Benefits:

This dua seeks contentment with Allah's provisions and trust that He will replace losses with better things.

Dua for Inner Peace

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ نَفْسًا بِكَ مُطْمَئِنَّةً، تُؤْمِنُ بِلِقَائِكَ، وَتَرْضَى بِقَضَائِكَ، وَتَقْنَعُ بِعَطَائِكَ
Allahumma inni as'aluka nafsan bika mutma'innah, tu'minu biliqa'ika, wa tardha bi qada'ika, wa taqna'u bi 'ata'ika
O Allah, I ask You for a soul that is at peace with You, believes in meeting You, is pleased with Your decree, and is content with Your gifts.
Reference: Common Islamic practice
Benefits:

This dua seeks inner peace through faith, acceptance of Allah's decree, and contentment with His blessings.

How to Use These Duas

Learn Gradually

Start with the most commonly used duas and gradually add more to your daily practice.

Understand the Meaning

Learn the English translation so you can recite with understanding and sincerity.

Practice Regularly

Make these duas a part of your daily routine to strengthen your connection with Allah.

Teach Others

Share these authentic duas with family and friends to spread the blessings.

Conclusion

These authentic duas from Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) are a precious gift that helps us maintain a constant connection with Allah throughout our daily lives. By incorporating these supplications into our routine, we can transform ordinary moments into acts of worship and remembrance.

Remember:

The most important aspect of making dua is the sincerity of your heart. Even if you can't pronounce the Arabic perfectly, Allah understands your intention and accepts your supplication. Start with what you can manage and gradually improve your recitation and understanding.
