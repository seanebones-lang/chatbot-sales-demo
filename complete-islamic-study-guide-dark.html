وَنَعُوذُ بِاللَّهِ مِنْ شُرُورِ أَنْفُسِنَا وَمِنْ سَيِّئَاتِ أَعْمَالِنَا

Welcome to the Islamic Study Guide!

The intention of this work was to compile as much information on the Deen as possible for easy access and search by anyone from a New Muslim, to anyone looking for information on Islam, or getting more specific information from the Holy Quran, all authentic Hadiths, and the complete Sunnah of the Prophet (صلى الله عليه وسلم).

In this application, Insha Allah, you will find many searchable databases, as well as guides for new Muslims, Daily verses from the Holy Quran that include duas for the day, and an inspirational message.

If any mistakes or misinformation is found please let us know. The intent is to provide only authentic verifiable information.

Thank you for downloading, and Insha Allah, you find this useful.

AI

DeenBot AI - Islamic Knowledge Assistant

DeenBot represents the integration of modern technology with traditional Islamic scholarship. This AI assistant provides access to authenticated Islamic knowledge, drawing from reliable sources including Quran, Hadith, and scholarly works. Each response is backed by authentic references, allowing users to verify information and deepen their understanding of Islamic principles.

Our intent Insha Allah, is for correct authenticated knowledge base. DeenBot can make mistakes, please verify the sources given. The DeenBot knowledge base must be able to answer modern questions from Muslims from around the world, every answer must be backed up with authentic source the user can verify.

Q

Complete Quran with Scholarly Tafsir

Access the complete Quran with authentic translations and scholarly tafsir (commentary). Our collection includes multiple translations to facilitate understanding across different languages and interpretations. Each verse is accompanied by scholarly commentary that provides historical context, linguistic analysis, and practical applications.

The tafsir collection draws from respected classical and contemporary scholars, ensuring that users have access to well-established interpretations while being aware of different scholarly perspectives within the Islamic tradition.

H

Authentic Hadith Collections

Explore comprehensive collections of authenticated Hadith from major sources including Sahih Bukhari, Sahih Muslim, Sunan Abu Dawud, Sunan Ibn Majah, Sunan Nasai, Musnad Ahmad, Riyadh Saliheen, and Mishkat Masabih. Each Hadith includes proper authentication chains (isnad), grading information, and scholarly commentary.

The collection emphasizes the importance of source verification and understanding the context of each narration. Users can access both Arabic text and translations, along with scholarly analysis that helps distinguish between authentic and weak narrations.

F

Comprehensive Fiqh Knowledge

Access complete Islamic jurisprudence covering all aspects of Islamic law including prayer, fasting, zakat, hajj, marriage, inheritance, business transactions, and contemporary issues. Our Fiqh database includes rulings from major Islamic schools of thought with proper scholarly references and contemporary applications.

The inheritance laws section provides detailed calculations and examples for proper distribution of wealth according to Islamic principles. All rulings are backed by authentic sources and scholarly consensus.
