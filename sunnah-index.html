Categories of Sunnah

Understanding the different types of Sunnah and their significance in Islamic practice and daily life.

Qawliyyah (Verbal)

The sayings and teachings of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, including his advice, explanations, and guidance on various matters.

Fi'liyyah (Actions)

The actions and practices of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, showing how to perform various acts of worship and daily activities.

Taqririyyah (Approval)

The Prophet's ﷺ silent approval of certain actions or practices, indicating their permissibility or acceptability.
