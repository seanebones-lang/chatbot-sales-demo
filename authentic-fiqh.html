Obligation of Five Daily Prayers
The five daily prayers (Salah) are obligatory upon every Muslim who has reached the age of puberty and is mentally sound. These prayers are: Fajr (Dawn), Dhuhr (Noon), Asr (Afternoon), Maghrib (Sunset), and Isha (Night).

Muslims must face the Kaaba in Makkah during prayer. If the exact direction is unknown, one should make their best judgment and pray. The prayer is valid even if the direction is slightly off.
