Surah Al-Falaq - The Daybreak

Surah Al-Falaq is one of the two surahs of refuge (Al-Mu'awwidhatayn) along with Surah An-Nas. It was revealed in Makkah and contains five verses that seek Allah's protection from various types of evil and harm.

Verse Information

Number of Verses: 5

Revelation: Makki (revealed in Makkah)

Position: 113th Surah

Alternative Names: Al-Falaq (The Daybreak), Al-Mu'awwidhah (The Refuge)

Virtues and Significance

  • Protection Surah: One of the two surahs specifically revealed for seeking refuge and protection
  • Morning and Evening: Recommended to recite in the morning and evening for daily protection
  • Before Sleep: Reciting before sleep provides protection throughout the night
  • Against Evil Eye: Particularly effective against the evil eye and envy
  • Black Magic Protection: Provides protection from witchcraft and sorcery
  • Complete Protection: Covers all types of evil and harm

Key Themes and Lessons

  • Seeking Refuge: Teaches the importance of seeking Allah's protection
  • Comprehensive Protection: Covers all types of evil and harm
  • Trust in Allah: Shows reliance on Allah as the ultimate protector
  • Spiritual Defense: Provides spiritual armor against various evils
  • Daily Reminder: Encourages regular recitation for continuous protection
  • Proactive Faith: Demonstrates proactive approach to spiritual protection

When to Recite

  • Morning and Evening: Recite three times each morning and evening
  • Before Sleep: Recite before going to bed for night protection
    • During Distress: Recite when feeling threatened or in danger
  • Travel Protection: Recite before and during travel
  • New Home: Recite when moving to a new place
  • General Protection: Recite anytime for spiritual protection
