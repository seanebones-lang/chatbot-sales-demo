Wudu (Ablution) Guide

Step-by-Step Wudu

1. Intention (Niyyah)
Make intention in your heart to perform wudu for prayer
2. Say "Bismillah"
Begin with "In the name of Allah"
3. Wash Hands (3x)
Wash both hands up to wrists three times
4. Rinse Mouth (3x)
Take water in mouth and rinse three times
5. Rinse Nose (3x)
Sniff water into nose and blow it out three times
6. Wash Face (3x)
Wash entire face from hairline to chin three times
7. Wash Arms (3x)
Wash both arms from fingertips to elbows three times
8. Wipe Head
Wipe head once with wet hands
9. Wash Feet (3x)
Wash both feet up to ankles three times
