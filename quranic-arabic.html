📚 Grammar Rules

🔤 Basic Sentence Structure

Arabic follows a Verb-Subject-Object (VSO) structure:

كَتَبَ الطَّالِبُ الدَّرْسَ
Wrote the student the lesson
Verb + Subject + Object

📖 Noun Cases and Gender

Arabic nouns have three cases and two genders:

Cases:
Nominative (مرفوع), Accusative (منصوب), Genitive (مجرور)
Genders:
Masculine (مذكر), Feminine (مؤنث)
