Prophet's Sunnah

Following the way of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) in daily life

Daily
Daily Sunnah
Morning to Evening Practices

The Prophet (PBUH) established a beautiful routine for daily living that brings blessings and spiritual growth. Following these practices helps us maintain a balanced and purposeful life.

These daily Sunnahs are not obligatory but highly recommended, as they bring us closer to Allah and help us follow the best example of human character.

Daily Practices

Morning Dhikr
Recite morning supplications and remember Allah upon waking
Miswak Usage
Clean teeth with miswak before prayers and meals
Dua Before Eating
Say "Bismillah" before starting any meal
Evening Remembrance
Recite evening supplications and Ayat Al-Kursi

Prophet's Teaching

"Whoever recites Ayat Al-Kursi in the morning will be protected until evening, and whoever recites it in the evening will be protected until morning."
Sahih Al-Bukhari

Benefits of Daily Sunnah

  • Increases blessings in daily activities
  • Strengthens connection with Allah
  • Improves character and manners
  • Brings peace and tranquility
  • Creates spiritual discipline
Eating
Eating Sunnah
Proper Manners at Meals

The Prophet (PBUH) taught us the proper etiquette for eating and drinking. These practices show respect for food, gratitude to Allah, and consideration for others.

Following these Sunnahs helps us maintain good health, show gratitude, and develop proper manners in all aspects of life.

Eating Etiquette

Start with Bismillah
Begin eating by saying "In the name of Allah"
Use Right Hand
Eat and drink with the right hand
Small Bites
Take small bites and chew thoroughly
End with Alhamdulillah
Thank Allah after finishing the meal

Prophet's Teaching

"When one of you eats, let him eat with his right hand, and when he drinks, let him drink with his right hand, for Satan eats and drinks with his left hand."
Sahih Muslim

Practical Tips

  • Always wash hands before and after eating
  • Share food with others when possible
  • Don't waste food - take only what you need
  • Eat together as a family when possible
  • Express gratitude for the blessing of food
Sleeping
Sleeping Sunnah
Proper Sleep Habits

The Prophet (PBUH) taught us the proper way to sleep, including supplications, sleeping position, and bedtime routine. These practices help ensure peaceful sleep and protection during the night.

Following these Sunnahs brings tranquility to our sleep and helps us wake up refreshed and ready to serve Allah.

Sleep Practices

Evening Supplications
Recite protective duas before sleeping
Sleep Position
Sleep on the right side facing Qibla
Clean Sleeping Area
Ensure the bed and room are clean
Morning Gratitude
Thank Allah for waking up safely

Prophet's Teaching

"When you go to bed, perform ablution as you do for prayer, then lie on your right side and say: 'O Allah, I surrender myself to You and entrust my affair to You.'"
Sahih Al-Bukhari

Benefits of Sleep Sunnah

  • Ensures peaceful and restful sleep
  • Provides spiritual protection during night
  • Helps maintain good sleep hygiene
  • Creates a mindful bedtime routine
  • Strengthens connection with Allah
Greeting
Greeting Sunnah
Proper Islamic Greetings

The Prophet (PBUH) taught us the beautiful Islamic greeting of "Assalamu alaikum" (Peace be upon you) and its response. This greeting spreads peace and creates bonds of brotherhood.

Greeting others with peace is a fundamental Sunnah that promotes harmony, respect, and unity among Muslims and with others.

Greeting Practices

Assalamu Alaikum
Greet with "Peace be upon you"
Wa Alaikum Assalam
Respond with "And peace be upon you too"
Greet First
Be the first to greet others
Smile
Accompany greeting with a smile

Prophet's Teaching

"You will not enter Paradise until you believe, and you will not believe until you love one another. Shall I tell you something that will make you love one another? Spread the greeting of peace among yourselves."
Sahih Muslim

Greeting Tips

  • Greet everyone you meet, even strangers
  • Use the full greeting: "Assalamu alaikum wa rahmatullahi wa barakatuh"
  • Greet children as well as adults
  • Maintain eye contact when greeting
  • Greet people entering your home or gathering
Work
Work & Business Sunnah
Islamic Work Ethics

The Prophet (PBUH) was a merchant and taught us the proper way to conduct business and work. These Sunnahs ensure honesty, fairness, and success in all our endeavors.

Following these work Sunnahs helps us maintain integrity, build trust, and achieve success while pleasing Allah.

Work Ethics

Honesty in Trade
Always be truthful about products and prices
Fair Treatment
Treat employees and customers fairly
Quality Work
Do your best work as if Allah is watching
Timely Payment
Pay workers promptly and fairly

Prophet's Teaching

"The truthful and trustworthy merchant will be with the prophets, the truthful, and the martyrs on the Day of Resurrection."
Sunan At-Tirmidhi

Benefits of Work Sunnah

  • Builds trust and reputation
  • Ensures long-term success
  • Pleases Allah and earns rewards
  • Creates positive work environment
  • Sets example for others
