Sahih al-Bukhari

The most authentic collection of Hadiths - Compiled by Imam Muhammad ibn Ismail al-Bukhari (810-870 CE)

About Sahih al-Bukhari

Sahih al-Bukhari is considered the most authentic collection of Hadiths in Sunni Islam. Imam al-Bukhari spent 16 years compiling this work, traveling extensively to collect and verify Hadiths. He only included Hadiths that met his extremely strict criteria for authenticity.

The collection contains approximately 7,275 Hadiths (including repetitions) and is organized into 97 books covering various aspects of Islamic life, from faith and prayer to business and social relations.

May Allah guide us to understand and implement these authentic teachings
