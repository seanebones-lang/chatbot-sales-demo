📖 Friday Reminder: Read Surah Al-Kahf

🌟 Benefits of Reading Surah Al-Kahf on Friday:

  • Light for the next Friday: Provides spiritual illumination for the entire week
  • Protection from trials: Shields from the trials of Dajjal (Antichrist)
  • Forgiveness of sins: Minor sins committed between two Fridays are forgiven
  • Increased blessings: Brings abundant barakah to your life
  • Spiritual elevation: Raises your spiritual status and connection with Allah

📚 Authentic Sources:

Hadith: "Whoever recites Surah Al-Kahf on Friday, a light will shine for him between the two Fridays."

Source: Sahih al-Jami' (6470), authenticated by Al-Albani
